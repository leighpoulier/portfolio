Cusco, Peru

Posted by Leigh on 8 Jan 2016

Cusco is old. Really old. The Killke people lived here from the 900s, then the Inca took over from the 1200s and it served as the capital of their empire. It’s also high, in a valley of the Andes and at an altitude of about 3,400m.
