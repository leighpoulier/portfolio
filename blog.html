In the Begining

Having been a life long tinkerer with all things computers, programming and electronics, i knew it would be the career i would choose if/when the time came for a career change. And after the last two years we have all had, you would understand how profoundly the time did come!

From the time i left high school, i pursued my other passion: aviation. I gained my Commercial Pilots Licence in 2006 at Moorabbin Airport in Melbourne, and began working as a flight instructor for the next few years, building up hours. In 2010 the big time hit, and I moved to Hong Kong to commence flying for a major international airline as a Second Officer, and later First Officer.

For the next 11 years I bounced around the world, to many major cities across Europe, Asia, Australia, Africa and North America, flying 747 and then 777 aircraft. Everything was awesome...

That is, until a certain bat got involved and put a stop to everything. The COVID-19 pandemic and its effects on my career gave me pause to reassess my life and its direction, and I decided flying was no longer for me. It was time to pursue my other passion: computers.

The next chapter

I am currently studing a Diploma of Information Technology at Coder Academy in Melbourne. This bootcamp style course will quickly give me the skills and experience to enter the industry as a junior developer, about which I am super excited.
